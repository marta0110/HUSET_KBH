Unique and original experiences since 1970

The house-KBH (formerly known as the house in Magstræde) is Denmark's oldest culture house with around 1500 annual events and larger festivals distributed on several scenes. Last year, the house attracted more than half a million. citizens and guests from home and abroad.

The house is a platform for many different cultural creators and, through volunteering and co-operation, offers a wide range of cultural features. These include musical live acts, performing arts, an extensive board gamescafé, atypical movie experiences and cult films, tasty food experiences, standup comedy, and poetry slam.

For four decades, the house has marked the Copenhagen underground and has been the arena for a number of prominent Danish and international artists. The house provides space for both the creative growth team, as well as professional performers in the field of music, film, performing arts and the spoken word and has been a framework for unique and original experiences since 1970.

The house is part of the City of Copenhagen, but our activities are mainly based on volunteering and co-creation as part of a larger unit of cultural houses around Copenhagen. The house administration is continuously working on initiatives and project proposals by volunteers and cultural entrepreneurs.
